Conversations

CoreConversations is the catalyst for difficult conversations about critical issues that impact our individual wellbeing but are of community concern.

Although we have choices in life, we seldom recognize that a choice exists as we reflexively live through conditioned responses.  Through video, we are working to activate change.   Our goal is to create videos that educate, encourage, and engage individuals on important choices.

Team

Most leadership work is done on an individual level.  We have taken the approach that individual leadership is enhanced in the context of the team.  That is why our conferences bring individuals together as a team to focus on specific objectives. 

The process enhances individual leadership, team effectiveness, and results in customized strategies to be immediately applied at various organizations within a community (schools, clubs, companies, city departments).
