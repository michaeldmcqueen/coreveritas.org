Misson

The mission of Core Communities is to expand knowledge, facilitate conversation, and inspire change within communities around factors that impact wellness.

Vision

The vision of Core Communities is that communities are empowered and driven by their members to pursue a shared goal of fostering wellness through collaborative conversation and action.

Content

Core Communities exists as a parent organization to power projects that strengthen community wellness initiatives. Videos That Matter is Core Communities' first collaborative venture that combines film, music, and animation to inspire a new approach to discussions around wellness.

Core Communities is a 501 (c) (3) organization founded in 2009 in order to increase public knowledge and involvement in community wellness. We believe that community wellness is a collective effort that begins with conversation and collaboration, which is why we created Videos That Matter to convey a visually compelling story about the needs in communities.
